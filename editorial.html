        My aim is always to create a brand identity that stands out from the crowd, that both captures the company's essence and appeals to their target audience.

        It's important that a brand's design is not just beautiful, but also encapsulates the company's core values and communicates them effectively.

        I ensure that the visual elements are consistent across all platforms and mediums to maintain a cohesive brand image.

        Project Development

        My approach to illustration is like weaving together a visual symphony. Everything starts by delving deep into the context, absorbing the narrative, and catching the emotional undertones. As the Head of Illustrators, I always instilled in my team the importance of this initial connection. Then, during the sketching phase, I play with compositions and experiment with forms and silhouettes until I hit the right note. I frequently shared this exploration with my team, inviting their perspectives and nudging them to stretch their imaginations. The final stage is a dance of colors and textures. Here, I pour in detail and depth, bringing the draft to life as a luminous work of art. This process is not just a personal journey for me but also one I've led my team through, watching as simple ideas bloom into evocative illustrations.
